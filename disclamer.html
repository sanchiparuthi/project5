DISCLAMER

The information contained in this website has been gathered and curated by interviewing multiple
children who are fed up of their caregivers' parenting skills. They all now claim to
resent their guardians for exhibiting certain behaviours and hope this project serves
not only as a guide on how to do better, but their experiences- as a cautionary tale to
prevent you from harming/ losing your kids mentally, emotionally or even physically.

The situations mentioned in the following piece are very common and humane experiences,
What is uncommon- is dealing with them/ supporting someone else dealing with them in sensitive, healthy ways.
That is precisely what this project aims to make the user think about.
The user is also requested to excuse the irritable tone of the piece.
Neither is this our job, nor should it be our responsibility but nevermind all of that.
Thank you. Happy parenting!
